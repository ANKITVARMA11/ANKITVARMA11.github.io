Performance analysis of flow-based Video Interpolation Techniques

Presented at Asia Pacific Conference on Innovation in Technology, July 2024
Co-author Dr.Harshlata Vishwakarma
Domain Computer Vision
Publisher IEEE Xplore
Visit (Publishing soon)

  • Performed detailed analysis of two fastest flow based Video Interpolation Techninques, RIFE and XVFI.
  • Interpolated same data of 9 different video formats at 3 different scales.
  • Evaluated performance by both general and metric evaluation.

  1. General Inspection
    • Time taken by each algorithm to interpolate the 9 video formats at 3 different scales.
    • Size variations of the input and output after interpolation.
  2. Metric Evaluation
    • Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio (PSNR)
    • Structural Similarity Index Measure (SSIM)
    • Video Multimethod Assessment Fusion (VMAF)
